Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Orders placed after 6:00 PM will be delivered the next day. We do not deliver on Sundays and public holidays.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12:00 PM. Orders placed after 12:00 PM will be delivered the next day.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of non-perishable items within 7 days of purchase with receipt. Perishable items can only be returned if they are spoiled or damaged upon delivery. Please contact our customer service for assistance with returns.

Do you source products locally?

Yes, we prioritize sourcing from local Filipino producers. About 70% of our products come from within the Philippines, supporting local communities and reducing our carbon footprint.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number on our website to monitor the status of your delivery in real-time.
