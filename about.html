Our Story

Nita's Minimart is filled with diverse and miscellaneous products that cater even to your specific wants. This is your one-stop shop for everyday supplies and niche items.

What started as a small venture has grown into a beloved community staple, providing quality products and excellent service to our valued customers. What was once Nita's Store became Nita's Minimart.

Llanita B. Briones

Owner & Founder of Nita's Minimart

Known for having a bubbly and warm personality. The minimart didn't start as minimart, instead, it started right at home. When the business became more successful, they expanded their store. The name Nita is the owner's nickname, hence the name "Nita's Minimart."
